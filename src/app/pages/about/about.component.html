A Few Words About Us

Features

  • Ofsted Registered Childminder with brand-new built facilities on the back garden purposely for looking after children;
  • Childcare services for 6 months to 8 years of age; Professional Members;
  • Association for Childcare and Early Years (PACEY) & Childcare.co.uk; Has a great combination of education, experience and brand-new facilities.

    • Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology Child Development;
  • Level 3 diploma for the children and young people’s workforce QCF (edexcel);
  • Level 2 certificate for the children and young people’s workforce QCF (edexcel);
  • Full Pediatric First Aid course (12 hours);
  • Coronavirus awareness;
  • Safeguarding duties and responsibilities;
  • Delivering EYFS;
  • Learning through play;
  • Communication and language;
  • Level 2 food and safety in catering;
  • Allergen awareness.

    • Setting

  • Child- safety large clean room separated from the house, wide range of books and toys for all age groups.
  • Curriculum tailored depending on the needs of the individual child following the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) as well as closely monitoring children’s development and their wellbeing;
  • Good size secured garden with a grass area and an outdoor playground;
  • Regular trips to the local park which is just opposite the house and to local play groups, libraries and so on;
  • A variety of carefully planned activities based on children’s needs to reach their full potential; A fun, happy, educational home to home environment.
